Do I need to register to use Launchlist?

Nope. We have a free version of the app available at http://lite.launchlist.net that you can use any time. If you want to take advantage of the full features of Launchlist, such as creating and saving lists, using templates and inviting other users you will need to sign up for a pro account. All accounts come with a 30-day free trial and we will never charge your account until the trial period has ended.

What are active checklists and templates?

Active checklists represent the ongoing projects that you're testing and haven't yet launched, whereas archived checklists are those which you've launched and/or closed. You're free to move checklists from active to archived (and vice versa) as you like. Templates are used to set up common lists for testing similar projects - from brochure sites to email newsletters.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We currently process all accounts through Paypal and accept all of the forms of payment that they allow - Mastercard, Visa, debit cards or even your bank. We do intend to offer credit card processing directly through the application at a later date.

Can I change plans? Is there a contract?

You're free to change plans at any time within the account screen of the application. Launchlist is a pay-as-you-go service and at no time will you have to have to commit to a contract. If you cancel your account you'll be billed for the current month, but you won't be billed again.

Logging in to Launchlist

When registering with Launchlist you'll nominate a Launchlist site address which will form the subdomain for logging in to your account. Once your account has been activated you'll also get sent an email confirmation.

Your site address will look something like this:

Just pop your this into your browser or enter your site address on the right.

If you've forgotten your site address and can't track down the confirmation email just send us an email and we'll be happy to help!
